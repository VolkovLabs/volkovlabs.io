# Business Links 1.2.0: Enhanced Navigation for Grafana Dashboards

We’re thrilled to unveil **Business Links 1.2.0**, the latest update to our powerful navigation panel for Grafana. Designed to streamline access to dashboards and external resources, this release introduces exciting new features and improvements to enhance user experience and customization.

Whether you're managing complex data visualizations or linking to critical business tools, Business Links 1.2.0 has you covered.

<Image
  title="The Business Links panel supports groups, dropdown and external links in Grafana."
  src="/img/blog/2025-04-28-business-links-1.2.0/panel.png"
/>

Here’s a quick overview of what’s new in this release:

- **Custom Menu Positioning**: Place navigation menus exactly where you need them.
- **Community Signature**: Officially signed as a trusted Grafana Community plugin.
- **Grafana 11.6 Compatibility**: Updated dependencies for seamless integration.
- **Enhanced Tab Sorting**: Prioritize selected tabs for quicker access.
- **Highlighting Options**: Easily identify the current dashboard or active link.
- **Hover-Based Dropdowns**: Access menus faster with hover functionality.
- **Built on Volkov Labs Templates**: A robust foundation for reliability and scalability.

## Why Business Links 1.2.0 Matters

Business Links is all about making navigation intuitive and efficient within Grafana. With version 1.2.0, we’ve focused on giving users more control over how they organize and interact with their dashboards and external links.

These updates empower teams to tailor their Grafana experience to their unique workflows, saving time and improving productivity.

## Detailed Release Notes

### New Features in 1.2.0

- **Custom Menu Position**: Choose where your navigation menu appears for optimal usability (#8).
- **Community Signature**: Now officially recognized as a trusted Grafana Community plugin (#10).

### Enhancements in 1.2.0

- **Grafana 11.6 Upgrade**: Updated dependencies to ensure compatibility with the latest Grafana version.

### Features Added in 1.1.0

- **Tab Sorting & Prioritization**: Sort tabs and prioritize selected ones for faster navigation (#6).
- **Highlight Active Links**: Visually distinguish the current dashboard or link for better clarity (#4).
- **Hover Dropdown Menus**: Display dropdowns on hover instead of click for quicker access (#3).

### Initial Release (1.0.0)

- **Foundation**: Launched the Business Links panel using Volkov Labs templates for a solid, scalable base.
