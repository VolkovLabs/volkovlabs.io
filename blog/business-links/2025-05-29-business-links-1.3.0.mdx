# Business Links 1.3.0: Enhanced Features and Grafana 12.0 Compatibility

We’re thrilled to unveil **Business Links 1.3.0**, the latest update to our versatile navigation panel for Grafana. Designed to extend Grafana's native link functionality, Business Links delivers unparalleled flexibility and customization, empowering users to effortlessly access dashboards and external resources directly from their Grafana environment.

## What's New in Business Links 1.3.0?

This release brings a host of exciting features, usability improvements, and full compatibility with Grafana 12.0. Here’s a glimpse of what’s in store:

- **Editor Icons**: Visual cues for intuitive navigation.
- **Flexible Layout Options**: Choose between horizontal and vertical grid layouts for a personalized design.
- **Time-Picker Input**: Streamline interactions with time-based data.
- **HTML Element Type**: Format content with enhanced structure and creativity.
- **Time Picker Highlight**: Quickly identify the active time picker.
- **Button Row for Dropdowns**: Improve usability with interactive dropdown menus.
- **Freeze on Scroll**: Keep the panel accessible on lengthy dashboards.
- **End-to-End Testing**: Ensure reliability with thorough testing.
- **Grafana 12.0 Compatibility**: Seamlessly integrate with the latest Grafana version.

Read on to explore each feature in detail and see how Business Links 1.3.0 can elevate your Grafana experience!

## Editor Icons for Intuitive Navigation

Navigating Business Links is now more intuitive with the addition of visual icons. These icons help users quickly understand the purpose of each element, enhancing the experience—especially for those new to the plugin.

Choose from an extensive library of predefined icons or maintain a unique style with the **Use Custom Icon** option to align with your branding or preferences.

## Flexible Horizontal and Vertical Layouts

Tailor the appearance of your Business Links panel with new layout options. Whether you prefer a compact horizontal grid or a more detailed vertical arrangement, these customizable layouts adapt to your dashboard’s design and usability needs.

## Time-Picker Input for Seamless Data Interaction

Simplify time-based tasks with the new Time-Picker input type. This feature allows you to interact with time-specific data directly within the Business Links panel, reducing clicks and boosting efficiency.

## HTML Element Type for Creative Formatting

Organize and present your content with flair using the new HTML element type. This powerful addition enables structured and visually appealing layouts for links and resources, giving you the freedom to get as creative as you’d like with HTML capabilities.

## Time Picker Highlight for Improved Visibility

Stay focused with the new Time Picker Highlight feature. By visually emphasizing the active time picker, this update ensures you never lose track of your current selection, enhancing both visibility and user experience.

## Button Row for Interactive Dropdown Menus

Dropdown menus are now more user-friendly with the addition of a button row type. This enhancement makes navigation smoother, more accessible, and visually engaging for all users.

## Freeze on Scroll for Long Dashboards

For those working with extensive dashboards, the new "Freeze on Scroll" feature is a game-changer. It keeps the Business Links panel fixed in position as you scroll, ensuring constant access to critical navigation tools no matter where you are on the page.

## End-to-End Testing for Unmatched Reliability

We’ve implemented comprehensive end-to-end (E2E) testing to guarantee the stability and performance of Business Links 1.3.0. With rigorous quality checks in place, you can trust that this update is built to deliver a seamless experience.

## Full Compatibility with Grafana 12.0

Business Links 1.3.0 is fully compatible with Grafana 12.0, incorporating updated dependencies for smooth integration and optimal performance. Stay ahead of the curve by leveraging the latest Grafana features alongside Business Links.
