# September 2024 Gazette: Business Suite Enterprise, 24 Million Downloads, and More

Welcome to the September 2024 issue of the Business Gazette! This month, we’re thrilled to share exciting updates and milestones with our community. Here’s what’s in store:

- [Business Suite Enterprise](#business-suite-enterprise) is now live and ready for new customers
- [Total Business Suite Downloads](#total-business-suite-downloads) have surpassed 24 million
- [Release Highlights](#release-news) from September
- [Blog Posts on Releases and Solutions](#blog-updates)
- [YouTube Tutorials and Shorts](#youtube-content)

Let’s dive into the key highlights of September!

## Business Suite Enterprise

Our open-source plugins stand out for their exceptional quality and reliability, making them a trusted choice for enterprise users in production environments. While we aim to address a wide range of use cases, we recognize that every customer has unique needs. That’s why we’re excited to introduce the **Business Suite Enterprise** program.

This program offers tailored support and benefits, including:

- A clear timeline for feature development and issue resolution
- Dedicated attention from a personal account manager
- Customized solutions for your specific use cases

Ready to elevate your experience? Learn more and subscribe via our [pricing page](/pricing/).
