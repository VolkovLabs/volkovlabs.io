# Business Intelligence 2.7.0: Smarter Profiles, Richer Previews, and Seamless Data Flow

We’re thrilled to announce the public preview of **Business Intelligence 2.7.0**, crafted by Volkov Labs. This release elevates the [Business Intelligence platform](/big/) with innovative features designed to streamline workflows and enhance productivity:

- **Personalized user profiles** for business and technical users.
- **Enhanced alert previews** with visible thresholds.
- **Refresh buttons** with 10-second auto-refetch for real-time updates.
- **JSON export/import** for effortless engine configuration.
- **Alert condition drawers** triggered by action events.
- **Infinite scroll** for seamless alert and action history navigation.
- **Performance boosts** through optimized database indexing.

## Tailored User Profiles Experience

Whether you’re a business decision-maker or a technical engineer, the new **business-oriented** and **technical** profiles adapt the platform to your needs—offering simplified dashboards for executives and detailed controls for tech experts.

Configure your profile easily via the icon in the top-right corner. Add your name, email (for support tickets), and select your role.

The **Data Preview** page now displays thresholds with Grafana’s intuitive color-coding, making it simple to identify trigger conditions at a glance. To explore this feature:

## Instant Insights with Alert Condition Drawers

Wondering why an alert triggered? Click any action event to open a drawer displaying the alert conditions at the time of firing—no extra steps needed. Here’s how:

1. Open action details.
2. Navigate to the **Action History** tab.
3. Click an event to view its conditions.

  title="Alert condition drawer provides instant clarity."
