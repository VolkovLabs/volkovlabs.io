# Business Intelligence 3.6.0: Automated Reporting, Smarter Alerts, Faster Insights

We’re excited to unveil **Business Intelligence 3.6.0**, a transformative update to the [Business Intelligence platform](/big/). This release turbocharges your Grafana experience with innovative features like advanced alerting, GPU-accelerated rendering, scheduled dashboard screenshots, enhanced Firestore security, customizable action templates, smarter alert logic, and refined UI for a seamless, accessible experience.

Ready to elevate your workflows? Let’s explore what’s new in this game-changing release!

Say goodbye to alert fatigue. Our **Smarter Alerting Logic** ignores "No Data" states when disabled, ensuring you only get notifications that demand action, cutting through the noise.
