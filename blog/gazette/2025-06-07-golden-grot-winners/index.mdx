# Golden Grot Awards 2025: Celebrating Winners Powered by Volkov Labs Plugins

The GrafanaCON 2025 stage was a showcase of brilliance, with the Golden Grot Awards spotlighting the incredible talent within the Grafana community. At Volkov Labs, we’re beyond excited to see our plugins enable winners for the second consecutive year. From personal passion projects to enterprise-grade solutions, these creators used our tools to transform complex data into visually captivating and highly functional dashboards.

## Unleashing Creativity with Award-Winning Plugins

The 2025 Golden Grot Awards winners, Ruben Fernandez and Grant Chase, showcased unparalleled innovation, addressing challenges from scientific research to global space analytics. Our **Business Text** and **Business Forms** panels, part of the Business Suite, offered the flexibility and power needed to bring their visionary dashboards to life.
