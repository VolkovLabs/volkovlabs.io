# Business Studio

Business Studio serves as the central control hub for the Business Intelligence platform, offering an intuitive and streamlined interface. It consolidates all critical configurations into a single, user-friendly application, empowering authorized users to manage their workflows efficiently.

With Business Studio, users can connect to multiple Business Engines—each linked to a specific Grafana instance—and seamlessly create and manage alert rules, reports, and actions.

## Downloading Business Studio

:::info Signed Releases
MacOS and Windows installers are digitally signed, notarized, and encrypted. The entire build process is fully automated via GitHub workflows to ensure the highest levels of security and integrity.
:::

Business Studio is a native desktop application compatible with MacOS, Linux, and Windows. Download the latest version directly from the [Release Notes](/big/release/) page or the [GitHub repository](https://github.com/VolkovLabs/business-intelligence/releases).

## Auto-Update Feature

Business Studio supports automatic updates on MacOS and Windows. The application checks for new versions hourly by querying the GitHub repository.

When an update is available, a dialog box appears, prompting users to restart the application and apply the update effortlessly.

## User Profiles Configuration

Whether you're a decision-maker or a hands-on engineer, our platform offers **business-oriented** and **technical** user profiles to match your needs—streamlined views for executives and detailed controls for technical experts.

Configure your profile by clicking the icon in the top-right corner. Enter your name, email (for support tickets), and select your role to personalize your experience.

<Image
  title="Easily configure your user profile."
  src="/img/big/business-studio/profile.png"
/>

### Light and Dark Themes

Business Studio automatically adapts to your system preferences, providing both Light and Dark color themes for a personalized and visually comfortable user experience. Themes can also be manually adjusted in the Profile settings.

## Streamline Operations

By offering seamless integration with multiple Business Engines and Grafana instances, Business Studio enables users to efficiently create and manage alert rules, reports, and actions from a single, intuitive interface.

With features like automatic updates, platform update notifications, and customizable themes, Business Studio ensures a modern, user-friendly experience tailored to diverse needs.

Whether you're using MacOS, Linux, or Windows, Business Studio provides a secure and reliable solution to streamline your business intelligence operations.
