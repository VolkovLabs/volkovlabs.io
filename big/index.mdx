# Business Intelligence Platform

Welcome to the **Business Intelligence (BI) Platform**, a cutting-edge solution crafted in collaboration with the Grafana Community. Built to address real-world production challenges, this platform revolutionizes alerting and analytics in Grafana through streamlined workflows, centralized management, and advanced features like AI observability and reporting.

Whether you're a business user seeking intuitive tools or a technical expert requiring robust functionality, our modular and scalable platform integrates seamlessly with all Grafana variants (OSS, Enterprise, and Cloud). Ready to elevate your Grafana experience? Let’s get started!

## Why Choose Our BI Platform?

Many users of self-hosted Grafana environments struggle with fragmented tools and limited advanced features. Through iterative design and invaluable community feedback, we’ve created a solution that tackles these challenges head-on by offering:

- **Centralized Control**: Manage multiple Grafana instances from a single, unified hub.
- **User-Friendly Design**: Intuitive interfaces accessible to users of all skill levels.
- **Scalable Architecture**: Modular components that adapt to your growing needs.

Our platform is built with modular components for maximum flexibility and performance:

- [**Business Studio**](studio/): A centralized management hub with an intuitive interface for system-wide oversight. Deploy just one instance to manage your entire platform.
- [**Business Engine**](engine/): Delivers high performance for individual Grafana instances by handling resource-intensive tasks. **Each engine supports a single Grafana instance**.
- [**Business Alerting**](alerting/): The inaugural module for the Business Engine, enabling query-driven alerts from Grafana panels with customizable thresholds and variables for precise, timely notifications.
- [**Business Reporting**](reporting/): Enhancing data insights and adding reporting capabilities.
- **Future Modules**: Planned innovations include AI observability, anomaly detection and other advanced tools to expand platform functionality.
- **Grafana Integration**: Fully compatible with Grafana OSS, Enterprise and Cloud. **Note that Grafana itself is not included in the platform**.

## Tailored User Experiences

The Business Studio is designed to meet the needs of two distinct user personas with specialized interfaces and tools:

### Business Users

- **Simplified Pages**: Streamlined, elegant interfaces tailored for internal teams or clients.
- **Core Focus**: Easily create alert rules, define actions, and conduct rapid analysis to pinpoint critical issues.

### Technical Users

- **Advanced Capabilities**: Comprehensive troubleshooting tools with access to payloads and Grafana data frames.
- **Target Audience**: Perfect for users with expertise in clusters and Grafana’s technical architecture.
