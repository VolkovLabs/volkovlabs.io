# Business Intelligence Platform

Welcome to the **Business Intelligence (BI) Platform**, a state-of-the-art solution developed in partnership with the Grafana Community. Designed to solve real-world production challenges, this platform transforms alerting and analytics in Grafana with streamlined workflows, centralized management, and cutting-edge features like AI observability and reporting.

Whether you're a business user looking for intuitive tools or a technical expert needing robust functionality, our modular and scalable platform integrates seamlessly with all Grafana variants (OSS, Enterprise, and Cloud). Ready to elevate your Grafana experience? Let’s dive in!

## Why Choose Our BI Platform?

Many users of self-hosted Grafana environments face challenges with fragmented tools and limited advanced capabilities. Through iterative design and invaluable community feedback, we’ve built a solution that addresses these pain points by delivering:

- **Centralized Control**: Manage multiple Grafana instances from a single, unified hub.
- **User-Friendly Design**: Intuitive interfaces accessible to users of all skill levels.
- **Scalable Architecture**: Modular components that grow with your organization’s needs.

Our platform is engineered with modular components for optimal flexibility and performance:

- [**Business Studio**](studio/): A centralized management hub with an intuitive interface for system-wide oversight. Deploy a single instance to manage your entire platform.
- [**Business Engine**](engine/): Provides high performance for individual Grafana instances by handling resource-intensive tasks. **Each engine supports a single Grafana instance**.
- [**Business Alerting**](alerting/): The first module for the Business Engine, enabling query-driven alerts from Grafana panels with customizable thresholds and variables for precise, timely notifications.
- **Business Reporting**: Enhances data insights with powerful reporting capabilities.
- **Future Modules**: Upcoming features include AI observability, anomaly detection, and other advanced tools to expand platform functionality.
- **Grafana Integration**: Fully compatible with Grafana OSS, Enterprise, and Cloud.
  - **Grafana itself is not included in the platform**.
